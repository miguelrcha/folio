---
title: Customization
description: Fine-tune any section of your profile without touching your GitHub data.
---

Every section of your profile — Overview, Experience, Stacks, Certifications, Languages, and Projects — can be edited directly from the profile page.

## Editing a section

Look for the pencil icon next to a section title. It opens a small editor for that section only; changes save instantly and show up on your public page right away.

## What's editable vs. generated

Overview, Stacks, and Projects start out generated from your GitHub data, but anything you edit by hand takes precedence — a resync won't overwrite a section you've customized.
