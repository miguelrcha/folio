title: Resume in PDF
description: One click turns your profile into a ready-to-send, single-page resume.

Every profile can be exported as a resume — same data, laid out for print.

## Exporting

Open your profile and click **View CV**. It renders a dedicated A4 layout, tuned to fit a single page for most profiles, and opens your browser's print dialog, ready to save as a PDF. A longer profile flows naturally onto a second page instead of being cut off.

## Customizing before you export

Signed in as the profile owner, **View CV** opens the CV Studio instead of printing right away — a dedicated screen to fine-tune the resume before it goes out: pick between the Classic and Modern templates, switch the font, show or hide your photo and bio, and toggle individual sections on or off. The preview updates as you go, since it's the same layout that gets printed — there's no separate rendering step to wait on.

Nothing here touches your public profile page. Changes stay local to your browser until you hit **Save**, so you can experiment freely — print or navigate away without saving and nobody else ever sees the in-progress version. On a phone, the controls and the preview take turns instead of splitting the screen: a floating button switches between them.

## Why it's separate from the profile

The resume uses its own typography and layout — it isn't just a screenshot of the site. There's no server-side rendering step involved, so it works the same wherever you host Folio.
