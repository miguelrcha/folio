Every profile can be exported as a resume — same data, laid out for print.

## Exporting

Open your profile and click **View CV**. It renders a dedicated, single-page A4 layout and opens your browser's print dialog, ready to save as a PDF.

## Why it's separate from the profile

The resume uses its own typography and layout, tuned to fit one page — it isn't just a screenshot of the site. There's no server-side rendering step involved, so it works the same wherever you host Folio.
