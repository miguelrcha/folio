Every profile can be exported as a resume — same data, laid out for print.

## Exporting

Open your profile and click **View CV**. It renders a dedicated A4 layout, tuned to fit a single page for most profiles, and opens your browser's print dialog, ready to save as a PDF. A longer profile flows naturally onto a second page instead of being cut off.

## Why it's separate from the profile

The resume uses its own typography and layout — it isn't just a screenshot of the site. There's no server-side rendering step involved, so it works the same wherever you host Folio.
