Folio turns your GitHub profile into a portfolio and a resume, with nothing to fill in by hand.

## Sign in

Click **Sign in with GitHub** and authorize Folio to read your public profile and repositories. That's the only step — there's no separate account to create.

## What happens next

Once you're signed in, Folio pulls your repos, the languages used in each one, and the stack icons from your `github.com/{you}/{you}` profile README (skillicons.dev, shields.io, or devicon badges all work). Everything gets combined into a profile at `meufolio.dev/{you}`.

## Keeping it current

Your profile resyncs automatically once a day. You can also trigger a manual resync from your profile page whenever you push something new.
