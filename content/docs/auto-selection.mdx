---
title: Automatic project selection
description: Folio ranks your repos by impact and pre-selects the best ones for your profile.
---

Not every repo belongs on a portfolio. Folio ranks your repos by impact — stars, activity, and how substantial the project is — and pre-selects the strongest ones to show on your profile.

## Overriding the selection

If you'd rather curate it yourself, open the **Projects** section on your profile and toggle any repo on or off. Your choice is saved immediately and reflected on your public page and resume.
