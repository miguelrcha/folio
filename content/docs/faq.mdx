### Is Folio free?

Yes — free while in beta.

### Is it open source?

Yes, the full source is on [GitHub](https://github.com/miguelrcha/folio).

### Is my GitHub token safe?

Your GitHub access token is encrypted at rest and only ever used server-side to sync your repos — it's never exposed to your public profile.
